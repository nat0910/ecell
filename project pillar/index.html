What is Project Pillar?

On a quest to look for worthwhile startups that hold the potential to revitalise the business industry by defying stereotypes and pursuing audacious goals. If you have what it takes to transform a good idea into a viable business with the assistance of a mentor/investor, apply for this event and find a pillar that will support your project to the heights you’ve always dreamt of.

Incentives

Make connections with VCs

Make connections with leading industrialists and venture capitalists who are willing to help you along the way.

Secure funding

Stand a chance to get hold of a potential investor and subsequently secure funding for your startup.

Consult with Mentors

Get the chance to consult with and acquire guidance from an esteemed mentor on our distinguished panel.

Secure your startup

Secure a position in reputable incubation that will support you along the way until your startup takes off and becomes successful.

Verification

Your pre-registration details will be verified and your startup particulars will be authenticated by our team. Fees will be charged after the authentication.

Workshop

In this workshop, you will get the required and effective mentorship to dive into funding strategies and pitching tips to attract mentors.

Mock Pitch

A practice/mock round where you will pitch your ideas to the mentors and improve your pitching skills under their guidance. be charged after the authentication.

Final Pitch

Rightly said by Winston Churchill, “The last part, the part you are approaching, it is the courage to continue that counts”. This round is the final round to pitch for funding and to turn your abstract idea and level it up into a worthy venture.
