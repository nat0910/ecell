What is Project Pillar?

We’re seeking startups that revitalise the business industry by defying stereotypes and that encourage budding entrepreneurs into courageous pursuits. Ideas come easy, the real challenge is to find financial aid, to look for necessary resources and eventually, the correct implementation. We aim to provide a platform for innovative ideas with promising future endeavours. A chance to get a hold of a potential investor or acquire the guidance of an esteemed mentor from our accomplished panel. If you have what it takes to turn a good idea into a viable business with the help of a mentor/investor, apply for this event and find a pillar that supports your project to the heights you’ve always dreamt of.
